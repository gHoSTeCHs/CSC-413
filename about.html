Do you find it challenging to figure out the starting time of your menstrual cycle? Well look no further because here at Flow-Cycle, We've taken care of everything for you.

Using the information you will be providing us, we should be able to perdict when your next period should occur. We ask for the first day of your last period, how many days it lasted and how long your cycle is.

Please note that cycle differ from individuals. The average cycle of an adult female is 28days and it can get as low as 21days or as high as 31days.

Having a period is note a bad thing but not being aware of your cycle can be pretty bad because you could get into an embarassing situation socially. Use our tracker to avoid such situations.
